Summary0000262: Window manager: Compiz-Reloaded conflicts with Plasma 5 taskbar switcher thumbnails
DescriptionPlasma taskbar switcher thumbnails broken. They were transparent, but now the bug seems to be that they are black most of the time, and the main window becomes white when they work. Disabling Plasma's taskbar thumbnails seems to work around the issue. Tracked upstream as https://github.com/compiz-reloaded/compiz/issues/26 (and probably https://github.com/compiz-reloaded/compiz/issues/25 ). Work in openbox, kwin, and compiz 0.9; broken in compiz 0.8 and compiz-reloaded

Video illustrating issue: https://archive.org/download/DSCN3602/DSCN3602.AVI
