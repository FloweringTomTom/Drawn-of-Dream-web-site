Futuramerlin Media

Page location: Home People Elliot Chandler

Futuramerlin Media (created 21 November 2016), a personal project of Elliot Chandler, is a publisher and distributor, primarily of music. Its catalogue includes a number of works formerly released by other publishers/distributors controlled by Elliot Chandler.
