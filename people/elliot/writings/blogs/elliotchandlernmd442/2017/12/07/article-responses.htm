Response to "The Future of UI / UX Design"

Article by David Issa: 18 April 2017, in Worry Free Labs

In the introduction to this article, the author writes that "the experience consumers have with your company is the product" (emphasis in original). This statement was quite striking to me. It makes me wonder whether because of this, non-profit organizations may supplant for-profit companies, especially Web companies, as Wikipedia (from the nonprofit Wikimedia Foundation) has done to some extent for encyclopedias. Nonprofits often have a much better public image than for-profit companies, and their Web sites generally have a much nicer user experience due to the elimination of intrusive third-party advertizing, being replaced by grants and donation funding.
