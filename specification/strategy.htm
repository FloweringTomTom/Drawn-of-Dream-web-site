Overview

This page documents the various real-world challenges involved in the project, and strategies for meeting those challenges.

Prerequisites

  • Financial support
  • Effective communication
  • Team building
  • Strategic planning

Roadmap

An initial goal should be to create a non-profit organization to manage and be responsible for the project, to ensure that organization's financial stability (through sources such as an endowment and grants), and to transfer control of the project's assets (Web presence, etc.) to that organization.
