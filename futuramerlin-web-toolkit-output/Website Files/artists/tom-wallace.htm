Biography
In addition to composing, Tom Wallace has performed, directed, taught, and coached since graduating with a Masters in Music from University of Maryland, College Park, MD in 1991. He spends his time composing and recording in his Southern Maine studio and working on the house. Since 2009, Tom has been collaborating on a full length musical, Charlie! In 2012 Tom released a CD recording entitled Drawn of Dream: Music for Massage, Yoga, and other Meditations. Songs of Loss & Love, words by Terry Quinn, is a Music Theater work still in development; it has been enjoyed in several concert and workshop productions in NYC. You can watch a short profile video of Tom working on a revision of Charlie!, created by Ari Erlbaum, at https://vimeo.com/127986449.

 A collection of songs, Nina's Lullabies, has been released, and a songbook for it, designed by Emily Bracale is in the works. Tom is currently fulfilling a commission for a CD of Christmas carols for harp, commissioned by Patrice Lockhart, and working on a second major work with librettist Terry Quinn, entitled Legendary Deaths.
