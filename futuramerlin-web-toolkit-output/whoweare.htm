Who We Are

At Lost Tree Music we get great enjoyment collaborating with each other and with guest musicians. At the core of the organization, Andy Johnson and Tom Wallace have been working together since 2009. Owen Wallace has recently joined us as a strong creative voice. Visit our Artists page to see people and groups that we have worked with, either in projects with Lost Tree Music, or in the past.
