Case Study: Project Roadmap — Elliot's NMD442 Blog!

[🕑: 2017m10d17]

Page location: Elliot's NMD442 Blog!

  • Due 9 Nov. 2017.
  • See case-study articles in Slack
  • End result is a "presentation packet"
  • Five minutes presentation
    • plus 5 to 10 minutes on questions
    • 15 min. total
  • Slides should be clean and simple.
  • Have our printed packets to hand out.
  • Articulate the user experience for the project.
  • Can extrapolate some aspects of the UX from simialr apps (e.g. Uber)
  • Target audience for this presentation is for investors / someone who wants to develop this project.
  • Prepare it for giving it to someone who wants to use it to develop an app.
  • Articulate enough that the person who gets the presentation/packet can have a good understanding of it and ask meaningful questions about it without having had us explain it to them, or without having us there to answer questions.
  • Comprises:
    • Research:
      • User interviews
      • Similar programs
      • Statistics
      • General knowledge of how plowing industry works
      • Business case — economics behind proposal
      • Bibliography — sources
      • Scenarios:
          • Technical issues
          • Technology
          • Locations (of audience of app)
          • Demographics
    • Use cases
    • Personas
    • Hypothesis
    • Visuals:
      • Wireframes
      • Story boards
      • Mockups
      • Prototype
      • Visual language?
      • Style guide?
    • Abstract
    • Project description
    • Budget
    • Elevator pitch / value proposition
